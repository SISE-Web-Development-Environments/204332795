ABOUT ME
who am I?
my name is Asaf Hadad and i am 27 years old.
I live in Beer-Sheva and for the past 7 years I work as a barber, therefore I have a lot of experience.
I own my own Barbershop in Beer-Sheva. I am vary passionate about my job! 7 years ago i started to cut all of my friends' hair and today my business has grown and expanded. My friends are still my clients even though I'm not giving them any discount- Im a professional barber so I can make a distinction between personal and professional issues.
As a hobby i like to sing, mainly middle Eastern music. im a big fan of Eyal Golan and i think he is the king of the middle Eastern music.
my dream is to open barbershop with Karaoke machine stand because i want to sing and do haircuts together.
